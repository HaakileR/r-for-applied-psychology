# Zenodo Description Draft

This repository contains open educational and research training materials for students of applied psychology and social sciences learning data analysis in R. The materials include a Quarto Book structure, synthetic Balkan-style teaching datasets, R scripts, lab exercises, solutions, reporting templates, and instructor materials.

The datasets are fully synthetic and created only for educational purposes. They do not contain real participant data and should not be interpreted as evidence about any real population, country, city, institution, or group.
