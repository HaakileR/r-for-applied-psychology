# Teaching Notes

## Recommended teaching approach

Use a gradual workflow:

1. Show the research question.
2. Inspect the dataset.
3. Explain what each row represents.
4. Run the analysis.
5. Interpret the result in ordinary psychological language.
6. Discuss limitations.

## Suggested class format

- 15 minutes: concept introduction
- 20 minutes: guided R demonstration
- 25 minutes: student practice
- 15 minutes: interpretation and reporting
- 10 minutes: common errors and questions

## Common beginner difficulties

Students often struggle with:

- file paths;
- missing packages;
- confusing numeric and categorical variables;
- interpreting p-values without effect sizes;
- writing results in plain language.

Use the exercises to slow down and connect every code line to the research question.
